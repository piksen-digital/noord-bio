Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Noord Bio and our platform.

What makes Noord Bio different from other biotech news sites?

Noord Bio focuses exclusively on applied nanotechnology and biotechnology with a startup-centric approach. We provide not just news but detailed analysis, technology readiness assessments, and curated investment opportunities. Our platform combines scientific depth with business intelligence for professionals and enthusiasts alike.

How often is the content updated?

Our content is updated daily with breaking news and weekly with in-depth analysis. Premium subscribers receive real-time alerts on funding rounds, clinical trial results, and regulatory approvals. We monitor over 500 sources including scientific journals, patent databases, and startup announcements to ensure comprehensive coverage.

Can I access Noord Bio on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Noord Bio is built with a mobile-first approach and works seamlessly on all devices. We also offer dedicated iOS and Android apps for premium subscribers with offline reading capabilities and push notifications for important updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for enterprise plans, we can arrange invoice-based billing. All payments are processed securely through PCI-compliant systems, and we never store your payment information on our servers.

Do you offer discounts for students or researchers?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount for verified students, academic researchers, and employees of non-profit research institutions. Contact our support team with proof of affiliation to get your discount code.
